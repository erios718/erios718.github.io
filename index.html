Security Priority

Have you ever been a victim of a cyberattack? Do you know anyone who has been a victim of a cyberattack? What is a cyberattack?


A cyberattack is defined as an attempt by hackers to damage or destroy a computer network or system. But a hacker can do much more. They can steal your sensitive information such as names, passwords, account numbers, social security numbers and much more.


On this site you will learn about past historical cyberattacks, types of cyberattacks, and how to protect yourself and view other resources.
